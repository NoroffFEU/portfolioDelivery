AuctionNest

AuctionNest is an interactive online auction platform where users can buy and sell items through bidding. New users receive 1000 credits upon registration. Registered users can manage profiles, track bids, view transaction history, list items for auction, and set starting bids and auction durations. Earn credits by selling items and use them to place bids on others. Real-time notifications keep users updated on bid status and auction results, ensuring a seamless and user-friendly auction experience.
